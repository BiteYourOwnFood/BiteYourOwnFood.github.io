Standard Steps

Lemonade Recipe

By Vinay Panchal

Ingredients

  1. Lemon 
  2. Water 
  3. Salt
  4. Black Pepper
  5. Sugar
  6. Soda (Optional)

Estimated Time

3-5 minutes

Estimated Cost

10-50 Rupees per serving

STEPS (Water Based)

Step 1

Take any clean container. Pour 150 ml of water and squeeze half a lemon(1 inch Diameter approx).

Step 2 

Add salt and sugar as per taste

Step 3

Taste a spoonful and add water or more lemon juice as per taste.

Serve 

Alternative STEPS (Soda Based)

Step 1

Take any clean container.Pour 150 ml of plain soda and squeeze half a lemon(1 inch Diameter approx).

Step 2 

Add just the sugar as per taste

Step 3

Taste a spoonful and add plain soda or more lemon juice as per taste.

Serve 

Keep Glass a bit empty as soda will rise after sprinkling salt and pepper

Add salt and black pepper just a moment before serving

Good if having with...

  • If having food that needs acids to regulate the digestion, then lemonade is best choice
  • Empty stomach also greatly beneficial

Tips

  • Add ice it up for better feel

Notes

  • Avoid copper, brass, bronze or silver utensils, as they can react with citric acid and cause side effects.
